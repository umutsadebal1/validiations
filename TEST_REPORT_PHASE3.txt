FIX #1 - XAML Parsing Error (Spacing attribute)
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Hata: "Cannot find resource named 'Spacing'"
Çözüm: VerifyTab.xaml'de tüm Spacing="value" yerine:

<StackPanel>
  <StackPanel.Margin>0,15,0,0</StackPanel.Margin>
  <!-- Elements -->
</StackPanel>

OR use Margin directly:

<StackPanel Margin="0,0,0,15">
  <!-- Elements -->
</StackPanel>
